I’m John  Rooney,
I solve  Problems

At The Law Office of John F. Rooney, V,  we help people in trouble. Simply put, we solve problems. Because of a desire to defend and pursue the rights individuals in need, John Rooney started his own law firm directly out of law school.

John surrounded himself with a number of experienced trial attorneys to serves as mentors in the fields of criminal defense and personal injury and set out on a mission to become a distinguished and accomplished trial lawyer. Today, at the Law Office of John F. Rooney, V,  we defend the rights of individuals who are facing prosecution in federal and state courts, and pursue the rights of individuals who have been injured by the negligence of others.
